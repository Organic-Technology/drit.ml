What is an eco friendly website ?

  • smallest energy footprint
  • minimalistic i.e. no bloated framework
  • no serverside processing (solely to manage inbound data)
  • serving only static resources such that they can be cached locally
  • deliver compressed version of files
  • operating on demand
  • loadable and operational with few kilo Bytes (reduced size images etc.)

More over at night time, we allow our *bot and souls to rest, so we scale down our operation to serve you with the strict minimum resources, while still giving you the attention to address your needs.

You can still have access to the full-fledged website from Cloudflare caches or better locally on your IPFS node if you have one.
