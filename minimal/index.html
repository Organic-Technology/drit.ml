What is an eco friendly website ?

  • smallest energy footprint
  • minimalistic i.e. no bloated framework
  • no serverside processing (solely to manage inbound data)
  • serving only static ressources such that they can be cached locally
  • deliver compressed version of files
  • operating on demand
  • a page of 1.97KB loadable and operational with 3.08KB of transfer (less than a 16KB packet size)
